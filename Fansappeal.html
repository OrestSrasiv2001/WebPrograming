I love this group. I listen to the songs regularly, it's nice to look at them, the lyrics are full of meaning, the music is rhythm, in general everything is harmonious))) Even though some Ukrainian words I couldn't understand))) But I learned through them this language. One of their songs "Without a fight" for a year is worth the call of a man, I can not listen. Under their creativity it is good to be in a state of love, to rest in body and soul. The guys are good, creative, creative, positive))) Music for the young and sick. Even a mom who listens and recognizes only the Time Machine loves a couple of their songs! For me, this group is a real discovery! I advise everyone that their music touches on for a long time.
