MiyaGi & Andy Panda is a Russian rap duo from the Ossetian city of Vladikavkaz formed in 2015. The team consists of two performers known as stage MiyaGi - Azamat Kudzayev and Andy Panda (Endgame) - Soslan Burnatsev.The members of the Ossetian national duo were born in Vladikavkaz, a city in southern Russia in the central part of the North Caucasus. Their creative biography is still small. Azamat Kudzaev, nicknamed MiyaGi (aka Shau), was born on December 13, 1990. He is the author of such famous songs as "Bonnie" and "In Your Love." Has been engaged in music for about ten years. He is considered the master of the chorus. He gave his first solo concert in May 2015. Soslan Burnatsev performs under the pseudonym Endshpil. Born October 2, 1995. He started rap at the age of sixteen thanks to his uncle, who introduced him to this line of music.

MiyaGi and Endspiel initially performed separately. But it's worth noting that in 2013 a joint album was released by Endgame.
